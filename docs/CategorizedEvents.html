Class: CategorizedEvents

CategorizedEvents() → {JSX.Element}

new CategorizedEvents() → {JSX.Element}

Renders - in a new page - a list of filtered events by a certain filter near the user's location. The available 8 filters are: 1- Food & Drink 2- Sports & Fitness 3- Health & Fitness 4- Performing & Visual Arts 5- Holiday 6- Business 7- Hobbies 8- Music
